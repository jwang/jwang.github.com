The following is a list of the resources I recommend for learning Objective-C, Cocoa and iOS programming.

These are to be used in addition to the Apple iOS Dev Center

Books

Objective-C

  • Apple's Learning Objective-C: A Primer
  • Objective-C Fundamentals - Christopher K. Fairbairn, Johannes Fahrenkrug, and Collin Ruffenach
  • Pro Objective-C Design Patterns for iOS By Carlo Chung
  • Pro Objective-C for Mac and iPhone - By Michael Ash, James Dovey
  • Learn Objectiveâ€“C on the Mac By Mark Dalrymple, Scott Knaster

iOS 4 Specific

  • iOS 4 in Action: Developing iPhone & iPad Apps by Jocelyn Harrington, Brandon Trebitowski, Christopher Allen, and Shannon Appelcline
  • iOS Recipes: Tips and Tricks for Awesome iPhone and iPad Apps by Paul Warren and Matt Drance
  • Beginning iPhone 4 Development: Exploring the iOS SDK - By David Mark, Jack Nutting, Jeff LaMarche
  • More iPhone 4 Development: Further Explorations of the iOS SDK By David Mark, Jack Nutting, Jeff LaMarche

Oldies but Still Good. These books reference older versions of iOS but are still decent resources for learning.

  • iPhone SDK Development by Bill Dudney and Chris Adamson
  • iPhone Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide By Joe Conway and Aaron Hillegass
  • iPad Programming: A Quick-Start Guide for iPhone Developers by Daniel H Steinberg and Eric T Freeman

Videos

  • Stanford CS193P course on iOS Programming. It is available on iTunesU as well.
  • Apple's WWDC 2010 video collection.
  • Apple's iOS Developmentvideo collection.

Blogs and Websites

  • Cocoa Is My Girlfriend - The blog of Marcus S. Zarra and Matt Long.
  • Cocoa with Love - The blog of Matt Gallagher.
  • iPhone Development - The blog of Jeff LaMarche.
  • Marco.org - The blog of Marco Arment.
  • furbo.org - The blog of Craig Hockenberry.
  • Matt Legend Gemmell - The blog of Matt Legend Gemmell.
  • Alan Quartermain - The blog of Jim Dovey.

For the Absolute Beginner

These resources are for those without any programming background and interested in learning.

  • iPhone and iPad Apps for Absolute Beginners By Rory Lewis
  • Hello! iPhone by Lou Franco
  • Apple's Your First iOS Application web document.
  • Apple's iOS Starting Point web document.
