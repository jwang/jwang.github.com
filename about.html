About Me

I’m a Web and Mobile Developer living in Honolulu, HI. I started making websites in 1997 with HTML and Javascript. I re-branded my freelance web design and development company from my personal name to Fresh Blocks LLC where I developed applications for the Apple iPhone and iPad. I have also worked on the JMAR project for the DoD and created internal web applications for Kaiser Permanente. A more in-depth list of my skills and work experience can be found on my LinkedIn profile or on my Resumé.
